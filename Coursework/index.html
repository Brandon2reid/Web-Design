Welcome to the Dueling Society

The Dueling Society is an unofficial society of RGU which focuses on challenging eachother to Yu-Gi-Oh duel monster battles. Whether you are doing official battles or just wanting to show off your cards, and even maybe do some trades and create new decks with the help of your fellow members you can! If this sounds like we are up your alley than come take a look and see if you would like to join and find your Exodia, we will welcome you with our decks ready.
